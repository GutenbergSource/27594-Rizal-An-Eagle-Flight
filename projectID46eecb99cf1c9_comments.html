This is a P3 Qualifying project. In P2 it is restricted to volunteers who have applied, or are thinking of applying, for access to P3, and it should be proofed according to P3 standards, including spellchecking.

In P2 please limit yourself to 15-20 pages per Qual. project.

This is the first (abbreviated) English translation of José Rizal's novel Nole me Tangere, originally written in Spanish. José Rizal is the Philippine national hero.

Another translation is already in PG as etext 6737. Having two different translations available is worthwhile. This translation is the only one Americans coming to the Philippines prior to 1912 had access to. At least three other translations to English have been produced, but these are still copyrighted. Also in project Gutenberg are a Tagalog and Dutch translation. A French translation is also in preparation.
